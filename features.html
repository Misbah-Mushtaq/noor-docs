Noor — Islamic Companion App
Features

Noor is an all-in-one Islamic companion for Android and iOS — prayer times, Quran, Dhikr, Zakat, Qibla, and more. Every feature is built for daily use with no account required.

🕌
Category
Prayer
Prayer Times
Accurate daily Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha times calculated by GPS location. Supports multiple calculation methods and Hanafi/Shafi madhab.
Home Screen
Optional Prayers
Ishraq, Chasht (Duha), Awwabeen, and Tahajjud times automatically derived from the day's prayer schedule.
Next Prayer Countdown
Live countdown to the next salah with the prayer name and time displayed prominently on the home screen.
Home Screen
Prayer Reminders
Configurable notifications for each salah — at time, 5, 10, or 15 minutes before. Per-prayer toggles for Fajr through Isha.
Prayer Journal
Log each prayer as performed, late, or missed. Review your consistency over time with a visual history.
Qada Tracker
Track missed prayers (qada) per salah with a daily make-up goal. Home screen widget shows total remaining and today's progress.

Quran
Full Quran Reader
All 114 surahs with Arabic text, English transliteration, and translation. Smooth scroll with per-ayah highlighting.

Bookmark any ayah and return to it instantly. Bookmarks persist across sessions without an account.

Focused view for hifz practice — hide and reveal ayahs to test recall, track progress per surah.

Quick access to commonly recited surahs — Al-Kahf (Friday), Al-Mulk, Al-Waqiah, and more.

A daily ayah with Arabic, translation, and surah reference shown on the home screen and as a widget.

Dhikr & Tasbih
Adhkar Sessions
Morning and evening Adhkar with Arabic text, transliteration, translation, and source. Ring progress per dhikr, auto-advances through the session.
Tasbih Counter
One-tap counter with quick presets for Subhan Allah, Alhamdulillah, and Allahu Akbar (×33). Cycles at 99 and tracks weekly total.
Custom Tasbeeh Targets
Create personal recitation goals with a name, optional Arabic text, and any target count — e.g. Al-Noor × 25,000. Progress persists across sessions.
New in v1.1.5
Dhikr Counter Widget
Home screen widget showing your current dhikr, session count, and total completed rounds.
