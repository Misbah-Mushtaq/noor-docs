Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 24, 2026

NOOR ("the App", "we", "us") is an Islamic companion app providing prayer times, Quran reading, Dhikr/Tasbih, Qibla direction, and related features. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device. The only information the App accesses is:

  • Location (approximate or precise): Used solely to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. Location is never stored beyond your current session and is never shared with third parties.
  • Device time & timezone: Used to display accurate prayer countdowns and the local clock.
  • Notification permissions: Used only to deliver prayer time reminders you explicitly enable in Settings. We send no marketing notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Computing prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) and optional prayers (Ishraq, Tahajjud, etc.) based on your geographic coordinates.
  • Pointing the Qibla compass toward Mecca.
  • Scheduling local prayer-time notifications on your device.

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond the core features listed above.

3. Data Storage

All app data — Quran bookmarks, Tasbih counts, Dhikr session history, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We operate no backend servers and hold no databases containing user data.

4. Third-Party Services

NOOR uses the following on-device or open APIs:

  • Quran API (alquran.cloud): Fetches Quran text and audio. No personal data is sent — only surah/ayah identifiers.
  • Prayer time calculation (Adhan library): Runs entirely on-device. No network request is made.

We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, social login, or analytics platforms (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK).

5. Children's Privacy

NOOR does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App contains no user accounts, no sign-up flow, and no mechanism to submit personal data. It is safe for all ages.

6. Permissions Requested

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Prayer times and Qibla. You may deny this permission; the App will ask you to enter a city manually.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Prayer time alerts. Entirely optional.
  • INTERNET: Fetching Quran text and audio from alquran.cloud.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Because we store nothing on external servers, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf. To erase all App data, simply uninstall NOOR from your device — this removes all locally stored preferences, bookmarks, and session history.

8. Security

Since no personal data leaves your device, there is no transmission risk. Local data is protected by your device's own security model (sandboxed app storage).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
